--- title: art, sound and assets description: tags: - section topic: content creation index: 1 --- # Art, Sound and Assets PlayCanvas supports the importing of a wide variety of content types: * Models (built in most mainstream 3D modelling packages). * Animations. * Images. * Audio samples. Once imported into your online project, these assets are made available to your project. The content creation guide shows how to: * [Build 3D models][build] so that they are PlayCanvas-friendly. * [Export 3D models][export] from applications like Blender, 3DS Max, Maya and others. * [Import assets][import] into your online project.
