--- title: content creation description: tags: - section topic: content creation index: 1 --- # Content Creation PlayCanvas supports the importing of a wide variety of content types: * Models (built in most mainstream 3D content creation packages). * Animations. * Images. * Audio samples. Once imported into your online project, these assets are made available to your project. The content creation guide shows how to: * [Build 3D content][build] so that it is PlayCanvas-friendly. * [Export 3D content][export] from applications like Blender, 3DS Max, Maya and others. * [Import 3D content][import] into your online project. Let's get started! [build]: /content_creation/building_content.html [export]: /content_creation/exporting_content.html [import]: /content_creation/importing_content.html
