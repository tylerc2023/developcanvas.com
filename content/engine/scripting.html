--- title: scripting description: tags: - section - engine topic: engine subtopic: scripting index: 2 --- # Scripting Introduction You can think of your application as divided into two separate code bases. The [Engine][engine_overview], which is provided by PlayCanvas, and implements general purpose functionality such as graphics rendering, input handling, audio, and the interface to the PlayCanvas tools; and Scripts which will often be specific to your application or re-usable chunks that provide useful behaviours.
