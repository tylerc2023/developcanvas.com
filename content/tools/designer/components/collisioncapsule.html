--- title: collisioncapsule component description: topic: components index: 6 --- # Component: collisioncapsule The 'collisioncapsule' component attaches a box-shaped collision volume to an entity. ## Properties
PropertyDescription
AxisAligns the capsule with the local-space X, Y or Z axis of the entity.
HeightThe tip-to-tip height of the capsule.
RadiusThe radius of the capsule's body.
## Scripting Interface You can control an collisioncapsule component's properties using a [script component](/tools/designer/components/script.html). The collisioncapsule component's scripting interface is [here](/engine/api/stable/symbols/pc.fw.CollisionCapsuleComponent.html).
